Impressions
a drinking game


Impressions is our newest drinking game that reveals what you think of your friends, what your friends think of you, and better yet, what you think of yourself.
Who gets friendzoned the most?
Who is most likely to say "I love you" and not mean it?
Who thinks of themselves as the funniest?
Who is the worst at drinking games?

Get a question and ask the group. On the count of 3, point to the person who fits the question. Block if you think that person is yourself.
