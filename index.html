First Impressions Gameplay

  1. Place the shuffled deck in the center of a table.
  2. Take turns drawing cards in clockwise order.
  3. When a player draws a card, he or she reads the card: "e.g. 'Who is most likely to get married first?"
  4. After reading the card, the player counts down "3, 2, 1, (zero)." (The zero is silent motherfucker).
  5. On zero, each player has two options:
    • Point - point to the person you think exemplifies the card (e.g. the person you think is most likely to get married first). The player who is pointed at by the most players loses the round, and takes the card (also takes a drink if playing correctly).
    • Block - if a player anticipates being pointed to the most (e.g. the player really is the biggest slut in the room), he or she can "block" by making an "X" with both arms in front of their chest. If a player blocks and more players are pointing to them than any other player, the blocking player avoids losing and can give the card to a player of their choosing. If the most players are NOT pointing at the blocking player, the blocking player loses the round and takes the card.
    Sudden Death: if two players qualify to receive the card (a tie), then the player who drew the card draws a second card and the turn repeats. This time, group can only point at one of the two players who tied for the first card. The player with the most fingers pointing at them gets BOTH cards. (Sudden death can repeat indefinitely until there is no longer a tie)
  6. The game ends when one player has received (x) cards or when everyone is too drunk.
